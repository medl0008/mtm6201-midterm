Get to Know Us!

About Us: Kneading Love is a local, family-owned business based in Ottawa, Ontario. What started as a passion during my maternity leave, has grown into a diverse, creative bakery experience, that offers unique and eccentric delicacies perfect for any occasion. We offer a wide-variety of treats, sweets, and delicacies perfect for any occasion and any hungry tummy. Our products meet the expectations of any event, whether it is a birthday, divorce party, or simply just the desire for something sweet and baked to perfection. We offer in-store, over the phone, and online ordering. Check out our product section to see how we can satisfy your sweet tooth, as well as your loved ones, co-workers, or anyone in your life who 'kneads' the perfect dessert.
